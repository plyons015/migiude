# Fair use policy (short)

Our **Power** plan is designed for heavy professional use. We provide very high monthly limits on cloud transcription and AI features.

**Fair use** means typical professional usage — full workdays, frequent meetings, and regular AI summaries. In rare cases of sustained extreme usage that affects service for others, we may reach out to discuss options (e.g. organization billing or adjusted limits). This helps us keep Ude sustainable and high-quality for everyone.

**Pro** plans use clear monthly caps (1,000 cloud transcription minutes and 200 AI actions per UTC month). **Free** plans use tight caps to keep costs near zero; unlimited **on-device** transcription remains the main free value.

Limits are enforced in Cloud Functions. See `lib/plan/limits.ts` for current numbers.
